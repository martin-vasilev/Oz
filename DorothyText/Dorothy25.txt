#####The Wizard now rose, took off the coat with the button heads, and
spread it on the floor, wrong side up. At once there crept from beneath
it a bear, a wolf, a cat, a weasel, and a field-mouse, who all rushed
from the room and escaped into the mountains.
#####'Come on, Toto,' said Dorothy; 'let's go back to the Emerald City.
You've given me a good scare, Wizard,' she added, with dignity, 'and
p'raps I'll forgive you, by'n'by; but just now I'm mad to think how
easily you fooled me.'
