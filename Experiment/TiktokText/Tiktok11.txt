#####Where Tiktok had stood was now only a scrap-heap and the Nome King
was so amazed by the terrible effect of his blow that he stared in
wonder.
#####His Majesty's anger quickly cooled. He remembered that the Clockwork
Man was a favourite subject of the powerful Princess, Ozma of Oz, who
would be sure to resent Tiktok's ruin.
#####'Too bad! too bad!' he muttered, regretfully. 'I'm really sorry I
made junk of the fellow. I didn't know he'd break.'
