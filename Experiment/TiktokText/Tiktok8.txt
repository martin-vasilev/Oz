#####Tiktok promptly walked in and faced the astonished Nome King, to
whom he said: 'Good morn-ing. I want two new steel springs for my
thought-works and a new cog-wheel for my speech-pro-du-cer. How a-bout
it, your Maj-es-ty?'
#####The Nome King growled a menacing growl and his eyes were red with
rage.
#####'How dare you enter my presence?' he shouted.
#####'I dare an-y-thing,' said Tiktok. 'I'm not a-fraid of a fat Nome.'
